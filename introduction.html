Entrance 1

As a team, our intention is to work together to achieve an optimal result. As a starting point for
you to meet us and trust the team, we have decided to define our important points when it
comes to organizing:
  1. Programming: A group of 8 members, well coordinated, that is working together  in the engine in order to reach points of innovation that live up to your expectations.
  2. Design: The design team is responsible for taking all that soup of tools and  innovations provided by the programming team and take out mechanics that every fan of the genre wishes
  3. Art: The three artists are responsible, through models and textures, to beautify the game. 
For future entries, we will emphasize in several specific aspects of each area: rendering
techniques (programming), fun theories (design), procedural materials (art), among many
others.
From the team we want the entries to be useful and interesting, as well as to gain a vote of
confidence. We will not disappoint you!
